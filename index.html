Leoneck Swiss Hotel

The Leoneck Swiss Hotel is decorated in a fancy ethnic design and is located at a prime location in the center of Zurich. A 6-minute walk from the train station, the Bahnhofstrasse, the banking district, the University and the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology and the major hospitals. Leoneck Swiss Hotel has 82 modern non-smoking rooms, all equipped with bath and shower. Internet is available free of charge throughout the hotel and the rooms are all furnished with air conditioning and a tablet PC. The restaurant 8001 named after the local postal code stands for modern Swissness. You can choose from our All American breakfast buffet, enjoy a varied business lunch, coffee and cake during the afternoon or savor our Swiss Tapas in the evening before an uncomplicated dinner with a delicious glass of wine.
